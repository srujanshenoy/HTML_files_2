FOOD ADULTRATION

  • ▶ it is the act of intensionally debasing the quality of food.
  • ▶ This is usually done by adding inferior substances or removal of some nutritional ingrediants.
  • ▶ While duing so, the food often does not hold up to meet legal standards and becomes unfit for consumption.

Ways to avoid food adultration
  • ▶ Check seal before buying products
  • ▶ Clean and store all grains, cereals, pulses, etc.
  • ▶avoid dark colored, junk, and other processced foods.
  • ▶ wash fruits and vegetable thoroughly in running water
